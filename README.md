# Habit Scorecard

A privacy-focused, intelligent habit tracker based on the "Habits Scorecard" concept from James Clear's book, *Atomic Habits*.

> **Live App:** [https://michaelp91-dev.github.io/HabitScorecard/](https://michaelp91-dev.github.io/HabitScorecard/)

## ‚ú® Features

This is not just a standard checklist. It's designed to help you become aware of your daily automatic behaviors.

* **Habit Scoring:** Mark habits as positive (`+`), negative (`-`), or neutral (`=`) to see your daily net score.
* **Smart Voice Logging:** Tap the microphone and say things like *"I drank a glass of water"* or *"Read for 20 minutes"*. The app uses a local semantic engine to understand you and log the correct habit instantly.
* **Hydration & Time Tracking:** Automatically extract quantities (oz, ml) and durations (minutes, hours) from voice commands or enter them manually.
* **Deep Analysis:** Get insights into your daily routine, including:
    * Net Habit Score.
    * Most active times of day (morning, afternoon, evening, night).
    * Total water intake vs daily goal.
    * Time spent on key categories (Exercise, Reading, Meditation, Chores, TV, Social Media).
* **100% Private:** All data, including voice processing, happens right on your device. Nothing is ever sent to a server.

## üì≤ How to Install

This app is a **Progressive Web App (PWA)**. You don't need an app store to install it; you can install it directly from your browser for a native app-like experience.

### On iPhone / iPad (Safari)
1.  Open the [Live Link](https://michaelp91-dev.github.io/HabitScorecard/) in Safari.
2.  Tap the **Share** button (the square with an arrow pointing up) at the bottom of the screen.
3.  Scroll down and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**.
4.  Tap "Add" in the top right corner.

### On Android (Chrome)
1.  Open the [Live Link](https://michaelp91-dev.github.io/HabitScorecard/) in Chrome.
2.  Tap the three-dot menu icon in the top right corner.
3.  Tap **"Install app"** or **"Add to Home screen"**.
4.  Follow the on-screen prompt to install.

### On Desktop (Chrome / Edge)
1.  Open the link in your browser.
2.  Look for a small install icon (usually a computer monitor with a down arrow) on the right side of the URL/address bar.
3.  Click it and select **"Install"**.

## üõ†Ô∏è Usage

1.  **Log your day:** As you go through your day, log your habits. You can use the dropdown list or standard voice commands.
2.  **Score them:** Once logged, tap the `+`, `-`, or `=` buttons next to each habit to score them based on whether they help you become the person you want to be.
3.  **Analyze:** At the end of the day, tap the **Analyze** button to see your scorecard summary and identify patterns.
4.  **Manage:** Use the settings (gear icon) to customize your default habit list.

## üîè Privacy

This application operates entirely client-side.
* Your habit logs are stored in your browser's `localStorage`.
* Voice recognition uses your browser's built-in Web Speech API.
* No personal data is collected or transmitted.
